# MTG Keyword Abilities

## Evasion (Affects Combat)
- **Flying**: Can only be blocked by creatures with Flying or Reach.
- **Reach**: Can block creatures with Flying.
- **Trample**: Excess combat damage beyond blocking creature's toughness is dealt to the defending player.
- **Menace**: Must be blocked by two or more creatures.
- **Intimidate** (obsolete): Can only be blocked by artifact creatures or creatures that share a color.
- **Fear** (obsolete): Can only be blocked by artifact creatures or black creatures.
- **Shadow**: Can only block and be blocked by creatures with Shadow.
- **Skulk**: Can't be blocked by creatures with greater power.
- **Horsemanship**: Like flying but only blocked by horsemanship (Portal Three Kingdoms).
- **Landwalk** (e.g., Islandwalk): Unblockable if defending player controls a land of that type.

## Combat Modifiers
- **First Strike**: Deals combat damage in the first combat damage step.
- **Double Strike**: Deals combat damage in both combat damage steps.
- **Vigilance**: Doesn't tap when attacking.
- **Haste**: Can attack and use tap abilities the turn it enters the battlefield (ignores summoning sickness).
- **Deathtouch**: Any amount of damage this creature deals is enough to destroy the creature it damages.
- **Lifelink**: Damage dealt by this creature also causes its controller to gain that much life.
- **Indestructible**: Can't be destroyed by damage or effects that say "destroy." Can still be exiled, -X/-X'd to 0 toughness, or bounced.
- **Hexproof**: Can't be the target of spells or abilities opponents control.
- **Shroud**: Can't be the target of any spells or abilities (including your own).
- **Ward [cost]**: Whenever this becomes the target of an opponent's spell or ability, counter it unless they pay the ward cost.
- **Protection from [quality]**: Can't be blocked, targeted, damaged, enchanted, or equipped by anything with that quality.
- **Unblockable** (not a keyword, but a common ability): Can't be blocked.

## Triggered Abilities on Entry/Exit
- **ETB (Enters the Battlefield)**: Triggers "when this enters the battlefield." One of the most powerful ability types.
- **LTB (Leaves the Battlefield)**: Triggers "when this leaves the battlefield."
- **Dies trigger**: "When this dies" — triggers when creature goes from battlefield to graveyard.
- **Undying**: When this creature dies, if it had no +1/+1 counters on it, return it to the battlefield under its owner's control with a +1/+1 counter on it.
- **Persist**: When this creature dies, if it had no -1/-1 counters on it, return it to the battlefield under its owner's control with a -1/-1 counter on it.
- **Afterlife N**: When this creature dies, create N 1/1 white and black Spirit creature tokens with flying.
- **Exploit**: When this creature enters, you may sacrifice a creature. "When you exploit a creature" triggers.
- **Modular N**: Enters with N +1/+1 counters. When it dies, put its counters on target artifact creature.
- **Evolve**: Whenever a creature enters the battlefield under your control with greater power or toughness, put a +1/+1 counter on this creature.
- **Fabricate N**: When this enters, either put N +1/+1 counters on it, or create N 1/1 colorless Servo artifact creature tokens.

## Activated Abilities
- **Tap abilities** ([T]: effect): Cost includes tapping the creature. Requires it to be untapped.
- **Sacrifice** ([Sac creature]: effect): Many engines use sacrifice as a cost or trigger.
- **Cycling [cost]**: Pay the cost and discard this card to draw a card.
- **Equip [cost]**: Attach Equipment to target creature you control. Sorcery speed.
- **Reconfigure [cost]**: Artifact creature becomes Equipment attached to target creature or detaches. Sorcery speed.
- **Unearth [cost]**: Return this card from your graveyard to the battlefield. It gains haste. Exile at end of turn or if it would leave the battlefield.
- **Morph [cost]** / **Megamorph [cost]**: Cast face-down as a 2/2 for 3 generic mana. Turn face-up by paying the morph cost.
- **Scavenge [cost]**: Exile this card from graveyard: put +1/+1 counters equal to its power on target creature. Land speed.
- **Transmute [cost]**: Discard this card: search your library for a card with the same mana value.

## Cast-Triggered / Alternative Cast
- **Flash**: Can be cast any time you could cast an instant.
- **Convoke**: Each creature you tap while casting this spell pays for 1 or one mana of that creature's color.
- **Delve**: Exile cards from your graveyard to pay for generic mana in this spell's cost.
- **Escape [cost] — Exile N cards**: Cast this from your graveyard by paying the escape cost and exiling N other cards from your graveyard.
- **Cascade**: When you cast this spell, exile cards from the top of your library until you exile a nonland card with lesser mana value. You may cast it without paying its mana cost.
- **Kicker [cost]**: Optional additional cost. If paid, the spell has an additional effect.
- **Overload [cost]**: Replace "target" with "each" in the spell's text. Changes it from single target to affecting all valid objects.
- **Replicate [cost]**: Each time you pay the replicate cost, copy this spell.
- **Splice onto Arcane [cost]**: If you cast an Arcane spell, you may pay this cost to add this effect to it (without casting this card).
- **Madness [cost]**: If you discard this card, you may cast it for its madness cost instead of putting it into your graveyard.
- **Flashback [cost]**: Cast this from your graveyard for its flashback cost. Exile afterward.
- **Jump-start**: Cast from your graveyard by paying its cost and discarding a card. Exile afterward.
- **Aftermath**: Split card where the second half can only be cast from the graveyard.
- **Foretell [cost]**: During your turn, pay 2 to exile this from your hand face-down. Cast it later for its foretell cost.
- **Suspend N — [cost]**: Exile this with N time counters. Remove one time counter each upkeep. Cast for free when the last is removed.
- **Surge [cost]**: Reduced cost if you or a teammate has cast another spell this turn.
- **Emerge [cost]**: Reduce cost by sacrificing a creature's mana value.
- **Mutate [cost]**: If cast for its mutate cost on a non-human creature, merge with the creature (put on top or bottom). The merged creature has all abilities of both.

## Graveyard-Matters
- **Dredge N**: If you would draw a card, you may mill N cards instead to return this from your graveyard to your hand.
- **Embalm [cost]**: Exile this from your graveyard: create a mummified white token copy of it with no mana cost.
- **Eternalize [cost]**: Like Embalm but creates a 4/4 black zombie token version.
- **Disturb [cost]**: Cast this card from your graveyard transformed.
- **Flashback [cost]**: (see above)
- **Escape**: (see above)

## Counter-Based
- **Proliferate**: Choose any number of players/permanents with counters. Put one more counter of each kind already there on each chosen permanent/player.
- **Infect**: Deals damage to creatures in the form of -1/-1 counters. Deals damage to players in the form of poison counters. A player with 10 or more poison counters loses.
- **Wither**: Deals damage to creatures in the form of -1/-1 counters (but not poison to players).
- **Poisonous N**: When this deals combat damage to a player, that player gets N poison counters.
- **Graft N**: Enters with N +1/+1 counters. When another creature enters, you may move a +1/+1 counter from this creature to it.
- **Vanishing N**: Enters with N time counters. Remove one each upkeep. When the last is removed, sacrifice it.

## Token / Multiplier
- **Populate**: Create a token copy of a creature token you control.
- **Amass N**: Put N +1/+1 counters on an Army token (or create one if you have none).
- **Myriad**: When this attacks, create a token of it attacking each other opponent (in multiplayer). Exile tokens at end of combat.
- **Encore [cost]**: Pay the cost, exile from graveyard: create tokens of it attacking each opponent. Sacrifice at end of combat.

## Utility
- **Prowess**: Whenever you cast a noncreature spell, this creature gets +1/+1 until end of turn.
- **Mentor**: Whenever this attacks, put a +1/+1 counter on target attacking creature with less power.
- **Connive**: Draw a card, then discard a card. If you discarded a nonland card, put a +1/+1 counter on this creature.
- **Investigate**: Create a colorless Clue artifact token with "2, Sacrifice this: Draw a card."
- **Improvise**: Each artifact you tap while casting this spell pays for 1 generic mana.
- **Crew N**: Tap any number of creatures you control with total power N or greater: target Vehicle becomes an artifact creature until end of turn.
- **Blitz [cost]**: If cast for blitz cost, it gains haste and "when this dies, draw a card." Sacrifice at end of turn.
- **Backup N**: Enters with N +1/+1 counters on it. Target another creature gets a +1/+1 counter and gains all abilities of this creature with backup until end of turn.
- **Bargain**: You may sacrifice an artifact, enchantment, or token as you cast this spell for an additional effect.
- **Enlist**: As this creature attacks, you may tap a non-attacking creature you control (not another Enlisting creature). If you do, add its power to this creature's until end of turn.
